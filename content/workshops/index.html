--- title: Workshops ---

The members of the Honeynet Project have been getting together for annual workshops since the early days. Those were private invite-only workshops during which members got together, exchanged ideas, coded and hashed out collaborative efforts for the next year.

Starting with our workshop in 2011, we have started to hold a security workshop for the public that consisted of presentations and hands-on exercises. This page contains links to our past public security workshops with links to presentations and videos. Enjoy!
